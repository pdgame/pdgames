Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. Learn how we protect your data.

Effective Date: December 5, 2025
Last Updated: December 5, 2025

Welcome to PDgames ("we," "us," "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe online gaming experience. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website https://pdgame.github.io/pdgames/ and use our services.

By accessing or using PDgames, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us, including:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, and phone number when you contact us through our contact form
  • Feedback & Communications: Any messages, reviews, or feedback you send to us
  • User Preferences: Game preferences and recently played games stored locally

1.2 Information Automatically Collected

When you visit PDgames, we automatically collect certain information about your device and browsing activity:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type and version
  • Usage Data: IP address, pages viewed, time spent on pages, click data, referring website
  • Location Data: General geographic location (country/city) based on IP address
  • Cookies & Local Storage: Information stored on your device for functionality and analytics

1.3 Third-Party Information

We may receive information from third-party services we integrate with, such as:

  • Analytics providers (Google Analytics, etc.)
  • Social media platforms (if you share our content)
  • Advertising networks (if applicable)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide, maintain, and improve our gaming platform
  • Personalization: To remember your game preferences and recently played games
  • Communication: To respond to your inquiries, feedback, and support requests
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with our site and improve user experience
  • Security: To detect, prevent, and address technical issues, fraud, and security threats
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms
  • Marketing: To send promotional content (only with your consent)

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

PDgames uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience:

3.1 Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic site functionality (cannot be disabled)
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and recently played games
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand site usage and performance
  • Advertising Cookies: May be used to show relevant ads (if applicable)

3.2 Local Storage

We use browser local storage to save your recently played games and preferences on your device. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

We may use third-party services that collect, monitor, and analyze information:

4.1 Analytics Services

Our site includes social media sharing buttons (Twitter, Instagram, Facebook). These services may collect information about your visit.

Some games on our platform are hosted by third-party developers. When you play these games, the third-party's privacy policy may apply.

5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third parties who help operate our website (hosting, analytics)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • Protection: To protect our rights, property, or safety, and that of our users
  • With Consent: When you give us explicit permission to share your information

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information:

  • HTTPS encryption for data transmission
  • Secure servers and hosting infrastructure
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication

Important: No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary:

  • Usage Data: Typically retained for 12-24 months for analytics
  • Contact Information: Retained until you request deletion or we no longer need it
  • Local Storage: Stored on your device until you clear it or uninstall your browser

8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

8.1 General Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Objection: Object to processing of your information for certain purposes
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw consent for data processing (where applicable)

8.2 GDPR Rights (European Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under GDPR:

  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority
  • Right to restriction of processing
  • Right to data portability

8.3 CCPA Rights (California Users)

California residents have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to say no to the sale of personal information
  • Right to access your personal information
  • Right to equal service and price

8.4 Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at:
